Hello!

My name is Abhinav Garg, and I am presently working as a Senior Analytics Fellow with McKinsey & Company, based out of the Gurgaon office. In this role, I have advised clients in more than 4 geographies on Risk Management and Analytics, with allied focus on Banking and Digital.
