Hello there!
My name is Abhinav Garg, and I am an Aerospace Engineering major at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay.
I started this web-page during the summers at the end of sophomore year. You can find on this page the courses that I have done so far (along with occasional reviews), details about the various internships that I have been through (this page owes a lot to them), and some chatter about the projects that I have done and would be doing.

Academics, I feel, is probably my forte, but I am actively involved in a lot of activities outside of it. I really like picking up new languages (programming and spoken likewise), writing (occasionally), playing the guitar and singing, and as for sports, I like to run and swim (though I am not great at either - they're still fun). You'll find links to my Facebook page (introduce yourself if you add me as a friend), Twitter account (I don't use it much), and Wordpress blog (I have bouts of extreme activity and total inactivity) under the aptly named Contact menu, along with my e-mail addresses. All in all, I hope you can find interesting stuff around here and have fun getting to know me or learning from my various escapades. Cheers!
